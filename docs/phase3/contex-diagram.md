# Design Documents
## Context Diagram
```
                     +---------------------------+
                     |                           |
    Students ------->|   Medical Submission      |-----> Submission Records
                     |   Management System       |
Medical Officer ---->|   (Sabaragamuwa Univ)    |-----> Statistics Reports
                     |                           |
                     +---------------------------+
```
### Explanation:
- **Students**: The primary users of the system who will submit their medical certificates for approval. They interact with the system to provide necessary information about their medical condition and reason for absence.
- **Medical Officers**: The users responsible for reviewing the submitted medical certificates. They will have the authority to approve, reject, or put submissions on hold based on the information provided by the students.
- **Medical Submission Management System**: The central system that facilitates the submission, review, and management of medical certificates. It handles user authentication, data storage, and processing of submissions.
- **Submission Records**: The database or storage system where all submitted medical certificates and their statuses are stored. This allows for tracking and historical reference.
- **Statistics Reports**: The output generated by the system that provides insights into the number of submissions, their statuses, and other relevant statistics for administrative purposes.
